Background

BTS World Tour Love Yourself: Speak Yourself was the stadium extension to the Love Yourself World Tour, separated under a different name. The extension began on May 4, 2019 in Pasadena, California and featured concerts in North and South America, Europe, and Asia, including the United States, Brazil, the United Kingdom, France, and Japan.
